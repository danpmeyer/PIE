Laryngeal Theory
The Three Laryngeals
h₁, h₂, h₃ — lost consonants with lasting effects
▾
PIE had three “laryngeal” consonants that disappeared in most daughter languages but left traces — colouring adjacent vowels and lengthening them.
h₂ colours *e → *a: *h₂ánti “in front” → Latin ante
h₃ colours *e → *o: *h₃rẖéƵs “king” → Latin rēx
Preserved in Hittite: *h₂ster- “star” → Hittite ḥasterz
